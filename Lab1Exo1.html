The World of Books

Why Read Books?

Books can take you to new places. They let you see the world differently.

Stories make you feel happy, sad, or excited.

Books and Knowledge

Books teach us many things. They help us learn and grow.

Reading helps us understand the world better.

Types of Books

Fiction

Fiction books tell stories that are not real. They are fun to read.

Harry Potter and The Hobbit are good examples of fiction.

Non-fiction

Non-fiction books are about real things. They teach us about life.

History and science books are examples of non-fiction.

The Joy of Reading

Reading for Fun

Reading makes us happy. It is a great way to relax.

Books Are Special

Friends in Pages

Books are like friends. They are always there for us.

There is always a new book to read!
